## English (en)

### App title  *(~9 chars)*
HackLampe

### Short description  *(~66 chars)*
Chop twice in the air to toggle your flashlight. Works even when closed.

### Full description  *(~1530 chars)*
Bring the famous Motorola "chop chop" gesture to (almost) any Android phone.

With HackLampe you just make two quick karate-chop motions in the air and your flashlight turns on. Two more chops and it's off again. No buttons, no fumbling in the dark, no unlocking your phone.

The gesture keeps working even when the app is closed. A lightweight background service listens for your chop and fires the torch instantly. You can also drop a Quick Settings tile into your notification shade to switch the whole feature on or off in a tap.

Every phone and every wrist is different, so HackLampe includes an auto-calibration wizard. It measures how hard you chop and sets the detection threshold for you. Prefer to fine-tune it yourself? A sensitivity slider lets you make the gesture as eager or as relaxed as you like.

HackLampe is built to respect you. It is fully offline, shows no ads, has no accounts and no tracking, and collects zero data. The whole app is around 0.9 MB.

Features:
- Double chop-chop gesture to toggle the flashlight
- Works in the background, even with the app closed
- Quick Settings tile to enable or disable instantly
- Auto-calibration wizard for reliable detection
- Adjustable sensitivity slider
- 100% offline, no ads, no tracking, no accounts
- Tiny download, around 0.9 MB

Requirements: Android 12 or newer, and a phone with a camera flash / flashlight.

Make light with a flick of the wrist. Get HackLampe.
